📘 Commonly Confused Concepts & Mnemonics

Types of Tenancy (Ownership)
  • Tenancy in Common:
    No survivorship. Unequal shares ok. Heirs inherit.
  • Joint Tenancy (PITT):
    Right of survivorship. Equal shares. Time, Title, Interest, Possession.
  • Community Property:
    Spouses (AZ). 50/50 ownership. Heirs inherit 50%.
  • Comm. Prop. WROS:
    Spouses. Passes to surviving spouse automatically.
Estates in Land
  • FREEHOLD (Ownership)
  • Fee Simple Absolute:
    Highest degree of ownership. Forever.
  • Fee Simple Defeasible:
    Conditional property (Determinable/Condition Subsequent).
  • Life Estate:
    Based on a life (Tenant or Pur Autre Vie). Reverts to Grantor or Remainder.
  • LEASEHOLD (Less than Freehold)
  • Estate for Years:
    Definite end date. No notice needed.
  • Periodic Estate:
    Month-to-month. Notice required.
  • Estate at Will:
    Indefinite duration. Permission granted.
  • Estate at Sufferance:
    Holdover tenant without permission.
The OR/EE Rule

Suffixes tell the story:

  • OR = Giver (ActOR, DonOR)
  • EE = Receiver (RecipEE)
  • MortgagOR = Borrower (Gives pledge)
  • MortgagEE = Lender (Receives pledge)
  • VendOR = Seller
  • VendEE = Buyer (with land contract)
  • OptionOR = Seller (Gives option)
  • OptionEE = Buyer (Receives option)
Valid Contract (COLIC)

COLIC

  • C - Competent Parties (Age 18+, Sane)
  • O - Offer & Acceptance (Mutual Consent)
  • L - Legal Purpose (Lawful Object)
  • I - In Writing (Statute of Frauds)
  • C - Consideration (Something of Value)
Debits vs. Credits

Closing Statement Rules:

  • Debit = Money you OWE / PAY 💸
  • Credit = Money you GET / SAVE 💰
  • Purchase Price: Debit Buyer, Credit Seller
  • New Loan: Credit Buyer
  • Earnest Money: Credit Buyer
  • Prorated Taxes: Debit Seller, Credit Buyer
Fair Housing (FRESH CORN)

Protected Classes:

  • Familial Status
  • Race
  • Equal (Sex)
  • Sex
  • Handicap (Disability)
  • Color
  • Opportunity (National Origin)
  • Religion
  • National Origin
Civil Rights Timeline
  • 1866: Race only.
    (Jones v. Mayer, No exceptions!)
  • 1968: Fair Housing Act.
    (Race, Color, Religion, Nat. Origin)
  • 1974: Added Sex (Gender).
  • 1988: Added Disability & Familial.
7 Protected Classes

"Realtors Can Really Sell Houses Fast Now"

  • Realtors = Race
  • Can = Color
  • Really = Religion
  • Sell = Sex (Gender)
  • Houses = Handicap (Disability)
  • Fast = Familial Status
  • Now = National Origin

Math Formulas
  • T-Bar Method:
    Part / (Total × Rate)

  • GRM:
    Price / Gross Monthly Rent
